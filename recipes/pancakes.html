Pancake Recipe

This is a great recipe that I found in my Grandma's recipe book. Judging from the weathered look of this recipe card, this was a family favorite.

Nothing says "weekend" like homemade pancakes for breakfast. Our easy recipe will help you whip them up in less than 30 minutes. Making pancake batter from scratch is so simple that you'll wonder why you never did it before!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg

  • 1/4 cup sugar

  • 1 cup milk

  • 1.5tbsp oil

  • 1tsp vanilla essence

  • 1 cup all purpose flour

  • 1 ts baking powder

  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, put the egg, sugar, salt, milk, and vanilla essence. Whisk it until a smooth batter appears

  2. Then add the flour and baking powder then whisk it until a real good batter appears (i dont know how tf to explain it make it and see it for yourself)

    Tip:If batter is too thick add more milk if its too runny add more flour

  3. Heat a frying pan on medium and wipe it with tissue and oil then take 1/4 cup of the batter then pour it on the pan then bubbles will start to appear when the bubbles stop appearing flip the pancake then wait 2-4 minutes until the other side is done then use up the rest of the batter (duh) then cook and serve the pancakes with honey or whatever you guys use for pancakes
